Coral Island Resort Hotels

City Club Hotel

A charming beachfront resort nestled on the white beach of Chalong Bay, Coral Resort provides rooms with private balconies. It offers a large outdoor pool, a diving centre and a massage pavilion

 

Coral Bay Hotel

Coral Bay Hotel provide your a riverside location, overlooking the Singapore River and Fort Canning Park. The stylish 4-star accommodations offer an outdoor pool, a gym and free internet in rooms.

 

Crone Plaza Hotel

Crone Plaza Hotel offers luxurious accommodations in the fashionable District. The elegant 5-star hotel offers free parking and free Wi-Fi access.

 

Grand Park Hotel

Luxurious living in the city can be found at Grand Park Hotel, a 5-star property with an indoor heated pool, 5 dining options and free Wi-Fi.

 

Singland Hotel
